<HowTo title="How to use a Signal username">
  1. Go to Signal > Settings > Privacy > Phone Number > and **set "Who can find me by number**" **to "Nobody"**.  This is important because you don't control the phone number you signed up with.
  2. [Set a Signal PIN](https://support.signal.org/hc/en-us/articles/360007059792-Signal-PIN) to help protect the phone number you are using in case someone else tries to register an account with that phone number in the future.
  3. [Create a Signal username](https://support.signal.org/hc/en-us/articles/6712070553754-Phone-Number-Privacy-and-Usernames#username_create).
  4. When people want to connect with you on Signal, give them this username instead of your phone number.
</HowTo>
