If you're at very high risk of being attacked for your politics, you'll want to make sure you've done a thorough job scrubbing your data online.

<HowTo title="">
  Start by upgrading your automated data removal tool from EasyOptOuts to something like [Optery Ultimate](https://www.optery.com/) ($250/year). It is among the most comprehensive automated opt-out tools. You can also consider [Kanary Pro plan](https://www.kanary.com/) ($10/month) for some additional coverage.

  Depending on your risk level, you may want to invest in a high-end service that can do thorough data scrubbing for you.

  Activist-aligned data scrubbing and doxxing response services:

  * [Brightlines](https://www.brightlin.es/)
  * [Convocation Research & Design](https://convocation.design/)

  You can also check out the [Access Now Helpline](https://www.accessnow.org/help/) which is usually free, but much slower and less thorough.
</HowTo>
