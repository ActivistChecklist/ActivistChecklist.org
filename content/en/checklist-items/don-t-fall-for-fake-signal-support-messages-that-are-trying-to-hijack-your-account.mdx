There have been an increasing number of [fake messages from "Signal Support"](https://netzpolitik.org/2026/phishing-attack-numerous-journalists-targeted-in-attack-via-signal-messenger/) that are attempting to hijack your account. 

Use these examples below to keep an eye out for fake signal messages. 

**Remember: Signal will never ask you send them your PIN or verification code.** It will only be asked of you when you are installing the app for the first time on a device. 

Read more about [Signal scams/phishing.](https://support.signal.org/hc/en-us/articles/9932566320410-Staying-Safe-from-Phishing-Scams-and-Impersonation)
