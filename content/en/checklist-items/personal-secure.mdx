We should assume that any government network or device can monitor anything we do, even if it is on our personal device.

**Recommendations:**

1. Do not bring your personal devices if possible
2. If you must bring your device, leave it off as much as possible
3. If you must turn it on, do not connect to government Wi-Fi (or ethernet)
4. If you must connect to the Wi-Fi, make sure you only do it with a trusted VPN active to shield your activity. Make sure to [install a VPN](/essentials#vpn) in advance.
