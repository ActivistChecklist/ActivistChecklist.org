---
title: Hide or deactivate social media accounts
slug: socials
preview: Helps protect you if they lookup your social media accounts online (without needing your device)
---

Unless you're intentionally trying to maintain a widely public social media presence, it's a good idea to lock down your accounts at least temporarily. (Consider doing it long-term for more privacy and security. Or just deleting the accounts outright if you can manage it.)

**Visa applicants:** If you are applying for a non-student visa, make sure you hide your social media before you apply. We know from [the case of an Australian journalist](https://www.newyorker.com/news/the-lede/how-my-reporting-on-the-columbia-protests-led-to-my-deportation) that waiting until the week before you fly might mean they have already looked you up online.

**Student visa applicants:** In June 2025, the State Department started [requiring all student visa applicants to make their social media profiles public](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/jun/18/social-media-student-visa-screening). If this applies to you, you may want to actually delete old posts using a tool like [Block Party](https://www.blockpartyapp.com/).

<HowTo title="How to hide or deactivate social media accounts">

Instructions:

1. **X / Twitter:** [make it private](https://www.lifewire.com/make-your-twitter-profile-private-3486093) or [deactivate it](https://help.x.com/en/managing-your-account/how-to-deactivate-x-account)
2. **LinkedIn:** [make it private](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a518980/) or [deactivate it](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1340802/temporarily-disable-account)
3. **Instagram:** [make it private](https://help.instagram.com/448523408565555) or [deactivate it](https://help.instagram.com/728869160569983)
4. **Facebook:** [make it private](https://www.guidingtech.com/hide-all-posts-on-facebook/) or [deactivate it](https://www.facebook.com/help/214376678584711)
5. **TikTok:** [make it private](https://support.tiktok.com/en/account-and-privacy/account-privacy-settings/making-your-account-public-or-private) or [deactivate it](https://support.tiktok.com/en/account-and-privacy/deleting-an-account/deactivate-your-account)
6. Consider hiding public other public posts like blog posts, YouTube videos.


Consider using [Block Party](https://www.blockpartyapp.com/) to assist with this process; [Cyd.social](http://Cyd.social) can also help you make a local, private backup first!

</HowTo>



<Alert type="warning">

While hiding your posts against the government might help you get into the US safer, we still need to keep speaking out publicly through posts, protests, and boycotts, because staying quiet might protect us individually but helps the authoritarian system we're fighting against.

</Alert>

You are not obligated to provide your social media handles to CBP (Customs & Border Patrol) or to the TSA. You can say that you are not comfortable answering or that you do not see the relevance of the question to your travel. Do not volunteer additional information for any question.
