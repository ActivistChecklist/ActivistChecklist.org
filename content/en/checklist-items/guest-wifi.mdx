This is a lower priority, but a good idea.

Enabling a "guest network" on your home Wi-Fi router ensures that your government devices don't have access to the personal devices on your network.

<HowTo title="How to enable and use a guest network">
  1. Look at your Wi-Fi router for a model or at least a brand
  2. Use [Brave Search](https://search.brave.com) (rather than Google) to search for `how to enable guest network on [MODEL OR BRAND HERE]`
  3. Follow the instructions you find and set up a Wi-Fi network with a different name than your main network.
  4. On your government phone and laptop, find the "Forget this network" option for your main Wi-Fi. Then re-connect them to the new guest Wi-Fi network.
</HowTo>

Wi-Fi routers can be difficult to configure sometimes. If you need help, ask someone tech-savvy in your life.
