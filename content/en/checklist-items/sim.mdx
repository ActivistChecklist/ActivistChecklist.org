---
title: Buy a SIM card
slug: sim
---

**If you bought a phone new directly from the prepaid provider: **it probably comes with a SIM card already. Read the box or ask whoever you're buying it from. If it has a SIM already, you can **skip to the next step**.

**If you are bringing your own phone, **you need to buy a SIM card.

<HowTo title="How to buy a SIM card">

You can buy prepaid SIM cards at the same place you can buy a phones and prepaid service plans: Best Buy, 7-11, etc.

Examples:

- [Tracfone's "bring your own phone" SIM kit costs $5](https://www.bestbuy.com/product/tracfone-bring-your-own-phone-mini-sim-pack-with-nano-micro-standard-multi/JXJCJWVFF4)
- [Mint Mobile's SIM kit costs $2](https://www.bestbuy.com/product/mint-mobile-prepaid-sim-card-starter-kit-gold/J3RZWKZGFK), but they also often have a SIM card + service plan bundles, so you could just buy that instead.

</HowTo>
