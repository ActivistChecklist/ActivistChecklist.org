[ICE is using Clearview AI](https://www.forbes.com/sites/thomasbrewster/2025/09/08/ice-to-pay-10-million-for-clearview-facial-recognition-to-investigate-agent-assaults/) to target people protestors and observers. Many law enforcement agencies also use this platform to do warrantless surveillance. 

You can protect yourself by opting out Clearview AI. (FYI - ICE is investing in [other facial recognition tools](https://archive.is/oldest/https://www.404media.co/inside-ices-supercharged-facial-recognition-app-of-200-million-images/) that you can't opt-out of.)

<HowTo title="How to opt-out of Clearview AI facial recognition">

[Submit an opt-out for form Clearview AI](https://www.clearview.ai/privacy-and-requests) - Requires sending a photo of yourself. 

Frustratingly, opt-outs are only possible in states that have passed privacy laws.

</HowTo>
