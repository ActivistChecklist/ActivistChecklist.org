title: Cover the front camera
titleBadges: []
---
If you were infected by spyware already, it can perform live surveillance on your device, accessing the camera and microphone. To limit what spies can see, it's a good idea to cover at least your front camera.

<HowTo title="How to cover your front camera">
  1. Get a sticker that has enough stickiness to be reusable. Or consider [this sticker pack from SLNT](https://slnt.com/products/webcam-cover).
  2. Put it on your phone and your laptop
  3. Move it off temporarily when you need the front camera, then remember to move it back.
</HowTo>
