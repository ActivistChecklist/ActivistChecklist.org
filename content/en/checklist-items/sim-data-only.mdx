<HowTo title="How to get a data-only eSIM or physical SIM">

1. Check [this list](https://www.airalo.com/help/about-airalo/NFHQSUXFCZOM/what-devices-support-esim/D2N6OZSVVM9W) to see if your phone model supports an eSIM or not.
2. If your phone supports eSIM, get a data-only eSIM from [Keepgo (North America)](https://www.keepgo.com/products/esim-canada-north-america-prepaid-data) (Or if you want to use Monero crypto for anonymity, use [silent.link](https://silent.link/)).
3. If your phone only supports physical SIMs, purchase one from [Keepgo (North America)](https://www.keepgo.com/products/lifetime-north-america-sim-card) and have it shipped to your location.
4. Follow the steps to activate your phone using this data plan.
5. Top-up the plan if you're running low on data. Make sure to check your data balance before you use it for a protect/action.

</HowTo>
