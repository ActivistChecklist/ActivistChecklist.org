---
title: Plan for emergency transportation and lodging (short- and long-term)
---

<HowTo title="How to plan emergency transportation and lodging">

1. **Transportation:** Identify how you will get to your safe location, including backup transportation options and the safest route to take.
2. **Short-term safe housing:** Confirm a specific place where you can stay safely for one night if you need to leave your residence quickly due to threats from non-state actors.
3. **Long-term safe housing:** Identify a place where you and any household members can stay safely for 1-2 weeks if you cannot return home for an extended period.

</HowTo>
