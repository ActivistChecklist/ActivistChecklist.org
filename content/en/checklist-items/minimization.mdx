---
title: Remove as many apps as can from your phone/device
---

If you want to make it tougher for a thief to get into your home, one easy step is to cut down on the number of doors. On your device, each app acts as "door" to the outside world, exposing you to increased risk of attack. If the app has a bug or vulnerability, it makes your whole device vulnerable.



<HowTo title="How to remove apps">

1. **Scan through all the apps on your phone** and computer.
2. **Decide if you truly need it.** Many apps can just as easily be accessed via their website instead. It is usually a little more inconvenient, but you get more privacy and security as a result.
3. **Uninstall it if at all possible.** If you're not sure, try uninstalling it for a week and see if you can manage without it. You can always reinstall later.

</HowTo>
