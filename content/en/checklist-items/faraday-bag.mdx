A faraday bag blocks all signals (GPS, cell, Wifi, Bluetooth, etc) coming in and out of your phone. 

In theory, turning your phone off should do the same thing. While not common, [there are ways](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/jan/27/nsa-gchq-smartphone-app-angry-birds-personal-data) to hack a phone so it can [connect to the network even if it is off](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/jan/27/nsa-gchq-smartphone-app-angry-birds-personal-data). A faraday bag provides an extra level of assurance.
