---
title: Disable voice assistants while your phone is locked (Siri or Google Assistant)
slug: voice-assistants-disable
preview: Protect yourself from the cops using your phone to do basic commands even when it is locked.
---

<HowTo title="How to disable voice assistants">

*On iPhone:* Settings > Siri > **Disable "Allow Siri When Locked"**



*On Android:* Settings > Google > Settings for Google apps > Search, Assistant & Voice > Google Assistant > **Disable "Lock screen"**

</HowTo>
