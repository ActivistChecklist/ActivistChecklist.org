[Observer Vault](https://github.com/lockdown-systems/observer-vault) is tool that lets you livestream video from your phone to your home computer simply by making a Signal call. It helps you make sure you have a copy of the video in case your phone is confiscated while filming.

You need an additional phone number to set it up, so it is for more advanced users.

<HowTo title="How to use Observer Vault for livestreaming">

1. **Get an additional phone number:** Options to consider: [Google Voice](https://workspace.google.com/products/voice/) (free), [SMSPool](https://www.smspool.net/) ($1 one-time per phone number), [MySudo](https://anonyome.com/individuals/mysudo/) ($2/month).
2. **Setup:** Install [Observer Vault](https://github.com/lockdown-systems/observer-vault) on your computer at home and follow the set up instructions. Test it.
3. **Keep your home computer powered on:** Make sure your home computer stays on and doesn't go into "hibernate" mode so it can receive calls. Check your computer sleep/power settings.

</HowTo>

*Note:* If you use SMSPool, make sure to turn on Signal registration lock and access the app at least once every 7 days. Otherwise someone else might register that number.
