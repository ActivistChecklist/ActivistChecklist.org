  Protect yourself from being targeted based on your social media content

---
We have seen multiple reports of DOGE using references to "DEI" on LinkedIn and other social media profiles

<HowTo title="How to set your social media profile to be private">
  **LinkedIn:**

  * **Public profile visibilty:** Go to LinkedIn > [Edit Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/public-profile/settings) > Edit Visibility > Either disable it entirely or select the items you want to disable to protect your privacy and history.
  * **Fully hibernate your profile:** If you are comfortable [fully hiberating your profile](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1336653/) to everyone (including those in your network) for a period of time, that is the most secure option. You may want to set a reminder to re-open it some time in the future.
  * **Tighten up other visibility settings:** LinkedIn > Profile Icon > Settings > Privacy > Visibility > adjust each of the settings to be more private.
  * **LinkedIn Activity Feed:** Your existing contacts on LinkedIn will still be able to see your feed. If you want to avoid targeting, you may want to [go through your history and delete posts](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1342513).

  **X/Twitter:** Follow [these instructions](https://help.x.com/en/safety-and-security/how-to-make-x-private-and-public) to hide your posts.
</HowTo>
