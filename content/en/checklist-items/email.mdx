<HowTo title="How to create an anonymous email account">

**Option 1: Use an email alias**

If you already have a Proton Mail account, you can use [their "hide my email" alias feature](https://proton.me/support/addresses-and-aliases#hide) (or any other email alias provider). 

**Option 2: Create a new email account**

1. Create a **separate email account** (we recommend [Proton Mail](https://proton.me/mail))
2. Use a [random username generator](https://strongphrase.net/#/username) to help this account be less strongly tried to your identity. (Again, we’re not aiming for total anonymity, but we are trying to create a bit of a buffer.)

</HowTo>
