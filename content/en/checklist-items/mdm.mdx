---
title: >-
  If you've ever had mobile device management tools installed on your personal
  device, remove them
titleBadges: []
---
Mobile Device Management apps are a piece of software that helps the government (or companies) remotely manage devices to keep them secure. If you were ever required to install one of these apps on your **personal** device, make sure to remove it if you're allowed to. This would usually occur when you're no longer employed by the government.

Common MDM apps:

* Microsoft Intune
* BM MaaS360
* VMware Workspace ONE (formerly AirWatch)
* MobileIron
* BlackBerry UEM
* Master Data Management by Civica

<HowTo title="How to remove mobile device management apps">
  1. Backup your device ([iPhone](https://support.apple.com/en-us/108366), [Android](https://support.google.com/android/answer/2819582?hl=en))
  2. Factory reset your device ([iPhone](https://support.apple.com/en-us/108931), [Android](https://support.google.com/android/answer/6088915?hl=en))
  3. Restore from the backup ([iPhone](https://support.apple.com/en-us/118105), [Android](https://support.google.com/android/answer/2819582?hl=en))
</HowTo>
