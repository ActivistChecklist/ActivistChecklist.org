If you are at high risk of arrest, detention, or right-wing violence, you may want to have a system in place to quickly notify your support network.

We recommend the [Circulo app](https://encirculo.org/en/) as a quick way to notify your network before you head into a higher-risk situation. You can securely send messages and your location to a select group of people.

Alternatively, you can create a Signal group and message each other updates.

Also, make sure you create a more detailed [emergency plan](/emergency/).
