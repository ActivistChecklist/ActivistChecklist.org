---
title: Designate someone who can access your residence and important items
slug: key-acces
---

Ensure at least one trusted person has access to your residence and knows where to find essential items in case you cannot access them yourself during an emergency. This may or may not be your primary safe person, depending on if they live near you or not.

<HowTo title="How to prepare someone for access to your residence">

1. Identify someone to access your residence and find essential items in case of an emergency
2. Give a key to trusted person or arrange key access
3. Show them location of important documents
4. Show them location of emergency money/resources
5. Show them location of medications and medical supplies

</HowTo>
