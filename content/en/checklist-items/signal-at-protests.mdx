<HowTo title="How to install and use signal">
  1. [Install Signal](https://signal.org/download/) on your phone
  2. Message or call your existing contacts using their phone number or ask someone for their signal username. They must have Signal installed also.
</HowTo>

Check out our [Signal Checklist](/signal) for detailed instructions on configuring Signal to be as secure as possible.
