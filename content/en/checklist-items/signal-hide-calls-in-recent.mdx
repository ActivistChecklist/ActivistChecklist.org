---
title: Disable "Show Calls in Recents" (iPhone-only)
titleBadges: []
---
This prevents the cops from seeing your Signal call history if they confiscate your phone.

<HowTo title="How to disable &#x22;show calls in recents&#x22;">
  This setting only exists on iPhones:

  * Signal > Profile picture > Settings > Privacy > Calling > Disable "Show Calls in Recents"
</HowTo>
