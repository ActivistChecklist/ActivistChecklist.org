**We keep us safe.** Surveillance relies on weak links. The more we each level-up our security, the more secure our entire community becomes.

**What is this flyer?** We created a flyer to bring to big public protests (like [No Kings](https://www.nokings.org/)) and community events so it's easy to spread the word about digital security and Activist Checklist.

**Why me?** If you've landed on this page, you're probably already more involved in digital security than 95% of the people in your community. You can be an ambassador to the rest of the people in your circles.

**Use the protest checklist, even for “easy” events.** Before you head out, run through our [prepare for a protest](/protest/) guide and encourage people in your circles to do the same. Why? A relatively low-risk march like No Kings is a chance to practice your security habits now! Building the muscle now means less scrambling when things heat up.
