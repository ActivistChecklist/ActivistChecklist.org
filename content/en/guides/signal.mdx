<Section title="Has Signal been hacked? Is it safe? Is it secure?" slug="secure">
  Every few months, we see posts circulate claiming that Signal isn't secure or has been hacked.

  **Has Signal been hacked?** **[Almost certainly not](https://hassignalbeenhacked.info/).**

  For a fuller explanation on Signal's security, including nonprofit ownership, encryption, open source, funding rumors, and device risks, read our article: [Is Signal secure?](/signal-secure/).

  There are risks to using Signal, but they are usually related to using it securely, rather than the technology itself. 
  
  For exmaple, if someone infiltrates a private Signal group, they could screenshot all of the messages and post them online.

  It's also possible that the cops can confiscate your phone and break into it if you have a weak passcode. That's not a failing of Signal, though. All your messages are available if anyone if they have physical access to your phone and can unlock it. That's why we recommend having a [strong passcode](/essentials/) and [always updating your device operating system](/essentials/).
</Section>
