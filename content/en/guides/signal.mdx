<Section title="Has Signal been hacked? Is it safe?" slug="secure">

Every few months, we see posts circulate claiming that Signal isn't secure or has been hacked.

**Has Signal been hacked? ****[Almost certainly not](https://hassignalbeenhacked.info/)****.**

What can happen is that someone infiltrates a Signal group and screenshots all of the messages.

It's also possible that the cops can confiscate your phone and break into it if you have a weak passcode. That's not a failing of Signal, though. All your messages are available if someone can unlock your phone. That's why we recommend having a [strong passcode](/essentials) and [always updating your device operating system](/essentials).
